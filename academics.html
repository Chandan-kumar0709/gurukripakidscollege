Academic Structure

Pre-Primary (Nursery & KG)

Our Pre-Primary section focuses on activity-based and play-way learning methods. The curriculum is designed to develop foundational literacy and numeracy skills, communication abilities, creativity, and social interaction. Students are introduced to basic concepts of language, numbers, colors, shapes, rhymes, storytelling, and motor skill development in a joyful learning environment.

Primary Section (Class 1–5)

The Primary stage builds strong academic foundations in English, Hindi, Mathematics, Environmental Studies, and Computer Education. Emphasis is given to conceptual clarity, reading habits, writing skills, logical reasoning, and discipline. Students are encouraged to participate in co-curricular activities to develop confidence and leadership qualities.

Upper Primary (Class 6–8)

At this stage, students are introduced to subject-oriented learning including Science, Mathematics, Social Science, English, Hindi, and Computer Studies. Practical understanding and analytical thinking are emphasized. Special focus is given to exam preparation, project work, laboratory exposure, and overall personality development.

Secondary (Class 9–10)

Classes 9 and 10 are conducted under Gurukripa Academy. Students are prepared according to CBSE curriculum standards with structured academic planning, regular assessments, and board-oriented preparation. Special attention is given to subject mastery, doubt clearing sessions, and disciplined academic training.

Smart Digital Classroom

Our school is equipped with Smart Digital Classroom where modern teaching tools such as projectors, multimedia content, and digital presentations are used. This interactive learning method helps students understand complex topics more effectively through visual and audio support, making learning engaging and practical.

Chemistry Laboratory

The school provides a well-equipped Chemistry Lab for practical learning. Students perform experiments under proper supervision to understand scientific concepts practically. The laboratory promotes analytical thinking, experimental skills, and real-world scientific understanding.

Computer Laboratory

Our modern Computer Lab provides hands-on learning experience in computer fundamentals, programming basics, digital literacy, and internet awareness. Students gain practical exposure to technology, preparing them for future academic and professional challenges.
